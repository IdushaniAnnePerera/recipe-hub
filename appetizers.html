Appetizer Recipes

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of baguette
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Toast the baguette slices until golden brown.
  2. Mix tomatoes, garlic, basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Top toasted bread with the tomato mixture.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 12 large button mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Remove stems from mushrooms and chop them finely.
  3. Mix chopped stems with cheese, garlic, parsley, breadcrumbs, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff each mushroom cap with the mixture.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes or until golden on top.
  6. Serve warm.

Chocolate Brownie

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Melt butter and mix with sugar and eggs.
  3. Add flour and cocoa powder and mix well.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes.

Fruit Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 1/2 cup orange juice

Steps:

  1. Chop all fruits into small pieces.
  2. Mix fruits with orange juice.
  3. Serve chilled.
